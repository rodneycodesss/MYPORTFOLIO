About

Rodney

Rodney Gilbert is a software engineer, web developer, and digital strategist passionate about building impactful tech solutions and modern online experiences. He specializes in creating responsive websites, custom web apps, and digital brand assets that elevate businesses and startups in Kenya and beyond. As the CEO of Rodstar Trading Agencies, Rodney leads a growing tech unit offering full-stack web development, automation services, and performance-driven social media strategies. He crafts user-centric platforms using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, PHP, and WordPress, while integrating back-end logic with Python and APIs. With a keen eye for UI/UX and digital branding, Rodney blends technical execution with creative strategy. Whether managing a web project, building a client portal, or scaling an online brand through Meta Ads and content marketing, he delivers results that bridge business and technology. Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, PHP, Python, WordPress, Canva, Meta Ads Passions: Digital innovation, youth empowerment, scalable software, and visual storytelling.
